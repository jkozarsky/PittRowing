    Pitt Crew greatly appreciates contributions, large and small, from alumni, parents, and friends. When all expenses are considered (boats, oars, transportation, uniforms, race entry fees), the costs of rowing are sizable. It is with your help that these costs are met, so we can row faster!


    Your donation to the University of Pittsburgh Rowing Team can be made by check or credit card payment, and if your company will make a matching donation, please notify your company's HR staff.


    Donations can be made online, by phone, or by regular mail. To donate by phone, please call 1-800-817-8943. To donate by regular mail, please fill out the Pitt pledge card. Make sure to indicate that you are donating to "Pitt Rowing Club".
